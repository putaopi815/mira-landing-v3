## Design Context

### Users

Hiring leaders and recruiting operators evaluating an AI-native, autonomous hiring system. They scan for credibility, system depth, and operational fit — not demo fluff.

### Brand Personality

Precise, calm, operator-trusted. Editorial confidence without startup hype.

### Aesthetic Direction

Light editorial / precision-product: generous whitespace, strong serif display, thin rules, labeled eyebrows, alternating narrative bands. Structural reference: [Aignostics](https://www.aignostics.com/) (section rhythm, typographic hierarchy, “Learn more” patterns) — **not** a visual clone; Mira palette and recruiting narrative remain primary.

### Design Principles

1. Hierarchy through type scale and spacing, not decoration.
2. One clear focal column; asymmetry where it aids scan (e.g. alternating bands).
3. Warm neutrals and existing accent — avoid cyan/purple “AI slop” gradients.
4. Motion: subtle scroll reveals only; respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.
